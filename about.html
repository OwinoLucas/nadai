GAMING SYNOPSIS

How it all started

In the 50's, a computer version of naught and crosses - or tic tac toe -was made available on one of Cambridge university's EDSAC computers. A decade later, a game called spacewar was made available to the masses, courtesy of a few brainiacs from MIT. Another decade later, the earliest known video game competition took place - a spacewars tournament. This sequence repeated itself exponentially - more advances in computer technology allowed fo a wider variety of games to be played on these machines. When the late 80's rolled by, it was too late to stop the wave of gaming that captured the attention of youths in developed countries. Japanese, American and European kids in this period had access to a fantastic variety of games - super mario bros, pacman, Doom, civilization 1, are all classics of late twentieth-century gaming.

Leaps in commercial technology in the twenty-first century allowed for gaming on a scale no one in the past decades could have foretold. Incorporation of the internet with gaming platforms allowed online role playing games to skyrocket in popularity and reach the level of fluidity you now enjoy. Now gaming is global, and can be indulged in by those in all sorts of locations - well, urban areas at least. Today, PC gaming is considered the technological pinnacle of the hobby(dont @ me, console lovers!), offering the very best in terms of graphical fidelity, atmospheric audio and immersive VR experiences. So keep this information in mind when you sit back and play your favourite video game. We've come a long damn way.

OUR HISTORY

Been in this game too long (pardon the pun..)

Nadai is a video game review site, based on the tastes of PC gamers on windows and linux operating systems. The site reviews games in the slim genres of sports, first-person shooters and role-playing games - however reviews for more "vanilla" games could be found on the website in times to come - including classics from the 80's and 90's

The founding developers of the site wanted to distinguish the site from those with similar review content. As such, a user-interface that is simple to navigate was prioritised rather than over-elaborate tools and features. Our site gives gamers what they need in the moment - quick and valuable tips and information regarding the game they want to investigate.

The next time you want to play a game yet aren't willing to look at pointless paragraphs of incomprehensible babbling, be sure to visit our website!
